# Stay Safe Online — Checklist (For College Students in India)

- [ ] Use strong, different passwords for each account.
- [ ] Turn on Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for email, social media, and banking apps.
- [ ] Do not click unknown links from emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.
- [ ] Install apps only from official app stores or trusted websites.
- [ ] Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts.
- [ ] Never share UPI PIN, OTP, CVV, or bank details with anyone.
- [ ] Check UPI collect requests and QR codes carefully before approving or scanning.
- [ ] Limit personal information shared on social media.
- [ ] Report cyber fraud at https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
- [ ] Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
